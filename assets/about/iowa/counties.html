Iowa has strict laws requiring that congressional districts be built out of counties. The county shapefile comes from the US Census Bureau's TIGER/Line Shapefiles. 2010 Decennial Census demographic data were downloaded from data.census.gov. County level election data for presidential elections since 2000 were obtained from the MIT Elections Data and Science Lab. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional informationabout processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
