In the 1920s, researchers at the University of Chicago divided the city into 77 community areas that have played an important role in city planning and culture since their inception and serve as proxies for neighborhoods. The community area and precinct shapefiles were downloaded from the Chicago Data Portal. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from data.census.gov. The source shapefiles with demographic information are available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization.
